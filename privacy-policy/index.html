Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

SMT Labs Studio builds mobile apps. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our apps.

Information We Collect

Our apps may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics about how you use the app
  • Crash reports: Technical information when the app crashes, to help us fix bugs
  • Local data: Information stored on your device to make the app work (never sent to our servers)

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use third-party services that collect information. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics)
  • Google AdMob

Advertising

Some of our apps display ads provided by Google AdMob. These ads may use cookies and device identifiers to show you relevant advertising. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

Data Storage

We do not operate servers that store your personal data. All app data is stored locally on your device. Third-party services (Firebase, AdMob) may process data according to their own policies.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
