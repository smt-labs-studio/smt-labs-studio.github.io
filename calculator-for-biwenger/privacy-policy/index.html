Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

SMT Labs Studio ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Calculator for Biwenger mobile application ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Authentication Data

When you log in to the App using your Biwenger credentials:

  • Email and password: Used only for authentication with Biwenger's servers. We do NOT store your credentials on our servers.
  • JWT Token: After successful authentication, a token is stored locally on your device to maintain your session.

1.2 League Data

The App retrieves and caches the following data from Biwenger locally on your device:

  • League information and standings
  • Player data and market values
  • Transaction history
  • Team compositions

This data is used solely to provide the App's functionality and is stored only on your device.

1.3 Log Data

We collect anonymous crash reports and error logs through Firebase Crashlytics to improve the App. This may include:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash logs and stack traces
  • General usage patterns (anonymous)

This data does not contain personally identifiable information and is used solely for debugging and improving the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate you with Biwenger services
  • Display your league data and perform calculations
  • Improve the App's performance and fix bugs
  • Provide customer support when requested

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is protected through the following measures:

  • Local storage: Your Biwenger session token and league data are stored only on your device using secure storage mechanisms.
  • No server storage: We do not operate servers that store your personal data or Biwenger credentials.
  • HTTPS: All communication with Biwenger's servers is encrypted using HTTPS.
  • No data sharing: We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

4.1 Google Play Services

Required for App distribution and updates. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

4.2 Firebase (Google)

We use Firebase for:

  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reporting and debugging

Firebase is subject to Firebase's Privacy Policy.

4.3 Biwenger

The App connects to Biwenger's API to retrieve your league data. Your use of Biwenger is subject to their own privacy policy and terms of service.

Note: We are not affiliated with Biwenger or AS.com.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally on your device and can be viewed within the App.
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the App will delete all locally stored data.
  • Revoke access: You can revoke the App's access to your Biwenger account by changing your Biwenger password.
  • Opt-out of analytics: You can disable analytics through your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored locally on your device. However, crash reports and analytics may be processed by Google (Firebase) in various locations. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
